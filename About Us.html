Who Are We?

Repaints Plus is an organisation that provides High Quality Repaints and Liveries for Virtual Planes in Prepar3d, X-Plane 11, and MSFS 2020. Repaints Plus is also begining to work on Airport Sceneries, That will end up providing a better Simulator Experience for your average Flight Simmer, that doesn't want to fork out 50 to 60 Dollars to make their Simulator look better.

Repaints Plus is a company that also goes above and beyond to help the community, as we are completely open source! If you dont like something in our projects, and you think you can help, Go Check out our Github, (here) and contribute to the project. We also value our customers feedback. If you have an issue with somehting that we need to fix, then Contact Us and we will strive to fix the issues!

Founder & CEO, Flynn Wright

As always, you can download our repaints and progress from the X-Plane Forums. We are active on the forums, so feel free to ask us questions there.

Donations are not required. These help the Repaints Plus Team, keep going, and helps us pay for the nessescary equipment for this, like Photshop, Substance Painter, and Sketchup Pro. Flynn Wright - Founder of Repaints Plus.
