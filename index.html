What Do We Do?

At RepaintsPlus, we are primarily focused on repainting aircraft for Flight Simulators, such as X-Plane 11, Lockheed Martins Prepar3d, and we are starting work on Microsft Flight Simulator 2020. Although our primary focus is Repaints, we are currently developing a Freeware Aircraft for X-Plane 11, which will have future compatibility with Flight Simulator 2020.

For all you need to know, Sceneries may be coming soon, but we cant promise anything. We run these Projects for free, and let the community use out assets, models and all, on our GitHub. Because this is a Free Project, we do have a donation link, meaning that Donations are greatly appreciated to keep the project going, but not nessescary.
